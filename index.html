Our Journey

A Story of Passion & Precision

Lumière Beauty Salon began as a small studio in the heart of the city with one simple promise: to deliver world-class beauty experiences in a warm, welcoming space.

Over 12 years, we have grown into a full-service beauty destination trusted by thousands of women. Our team has trained across Mumbai, Delhi, London, and Seoul — bringing the latest global techniques to every appointment.

We believe beauty is not about conforming to trends — it's about feeling deeply, genuinely yourself. Every service we offer is designed to enhance your unique features and boost your confidence.
